top of `v0.2.0-rc2`.

- Generated Results preview.
- Reliable local DBML diagram preview.
- dbdiagram.io is secondary.
- No backend/artifact contract changes.
- Validation: pytest 79 passed, 3 skipped; Ruff; Playwright; demo-full;
